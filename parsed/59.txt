It was founded in the late 19 century. Today, Botanic Garden is one of the scientific departments of the university. In relatively small areas concentrated live collection. Among all the exotic trees currently present at the Garden, manifolds are of a special value. They have survived since the Garden onset and now make up near 10% of the collection. The are Taxodium distichum Rich., Taxus baccata L., Liriodendron tulipifera L. etc. Particular attention should be paid to unique for our region Cryptomeria japonica Don., Kuninhamiya lanceolata, Sequoiadendron giganteum, Decaisnea fargesii, as well as representatives of the family Styracaceae and very rarely in Ukraine Gardens.
