The Dawes Arboretum presently hosts four Accredited National Plant Collections, under the auspices of the American Public Gardens Association's Plant Collection Network (https:	//www.publicgardens.org/programs/about-plant-collections-network). These include Acer (maple, multi-institutional), Metasequoia (dawn redwood, genetic and cultivars), Aesculus (buckeyes and horse-chestnuts) and Hamamelis (witch-hazel).
