Info:	  Brazil - Rio Grande do Sul - Lajeado  ;Garden Information;  Institution Code: LAJ; International Agenda Registration: Yes; BGCI Member: No; About the Lajeado Botanic Garden Lageado´s Botanic Garden, is located in Lajeado City, in the brazilian state of Rio Grande do Sul, south of the country. It was created in June 24 of 1996, by the means of the municipal law n° 5.721, with 26 hectares the local was considered by the director plan of the city as an area of enviromental priority for conservation. Today the Botanic garden is linked to de Enviromental Education Center by the means of the Minicipal Decret n°6.330, of 17 June of 2005. The transformation of the area from an old farm, brought the prospect of an adicional instrument for recovery and preservation of local flora and faunae, preventing hunting and removal of specimens. ; The natural processes of natural recovery of the vegetation, is taking place, making the landscape with areas with diferent levels of plant sucession. In ten years we are able to see the consolidation of the understory, and recovery of the before, degradated areas, as the result of the politcs for manteining the area as a conservational priority. ; Scientific studies took place in the area and were the base for creating the zoning of diferent use areas, each one with different management strategies.
