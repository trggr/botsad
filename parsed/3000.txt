It was created in June 24 of 1996, by the means of the municipal law n° 5.721, with 26 hectares the local was considered by the director plan of the city as an area of enviromental priority for conservation. Today the Botanic garden is linked to de Enviromental Education Center by the means of the Minicipal Decret n°6.330, of 17 June of 2005. The transformation of the area from an old farm, brought the prospect of an adicional instrument for recovery and preservation of local flora and faunae, preventing hunting and removal of specimens. ;  The natural processes of natural recovery of the vegetation, is taking place, making the landscape with areas with diferent levels of plant sucession.
