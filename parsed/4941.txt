9-About the Joan of Arc Garden        The year 2013 marks the 75th anniversary of the Joan of Arc Garden, the Battlefields Park’s gem. Created in 1938 by landscape architect Louis Perron, this garden combines French classic style with English-style mixed borders. More than 150 species can be admired: annual flowers and bulbous plants but most of all, perennials.
