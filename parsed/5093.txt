In 2011, he was granted the status of Botanical Garden and member of the Council of Botanic Gardens and Arboretums in Poland. The garden is primarily gathering medicinal and aromatic plants. Currently, it has about 1,500 taxa, including 85 species with strict and 13 partial statutory protection. Collection material is collected both in systematic order, according to belonging to botanical families, and taking into account habitat requirements (plants of arid lands, forests, meadows, wetlands and water areas).;  ;  The specifics of the Garden are collections of populations of wild growing medicinal plants obtained from the area of ​​Eastern Poland, being research material used, among others by scientific institutions cooperating with the Garden. The garden regularly publishes the Index Seminum and conducts exchange of seed material with 45 national and foreign institutions. In 2012, the Garden of Nature Education was established at the Garden, conducting classes for children, school youth and university students.
