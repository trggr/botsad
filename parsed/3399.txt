"garden-photo"> <img src="/photos/3399/Garden TMBU.png" alt="Main gate of Bhagalpur University Botanical Garden" /> Main gate of Bhagalpur University Botanical Garden  About the Bhagalpur University Botanical Garden The Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University lies between:	25.1400N Latitude and 86.37E Longitudes. The total area of the T. M. Bhagalpur University campus is about 264 acres. The University is located about 3 km away from Bhagalpur railway station at the bank of the River Ganga. The Bhagalpur University Botanical garden is situated on the campus of the University Department of Botany (25.1400 N Latitude and 86.5600E Longitude). The garden has a beautiful landscape and covers a total area of about eight acres of land. It is internationally recognized and available in Botanical Garden Conservation International (BGCI) and Indian Botanical Garden Network (IBGN) website. Recently, the garden has taken part in the Global Survey of Ex situ Ebony Collections of Missouri Botanical Garden and BGCI and acknowledged for the same. A large number of plants including some rare, endangered, threatened and endemic (RET &E) i.e. plants genetic resources are being conserved at present in the Bhagalpur University Botanic Garden. Some of the sacred plants are worshiped by the locals. The Botanic garden is organized various education & awareness activities such as seminars, symposia, nature camps, nature trails, etc. for awaring the students and people about the importance of conserving the rare and threatened plant species.
