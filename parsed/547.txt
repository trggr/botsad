The Park is home to the only tract of rainforest within the City comprising of native species sourced across PNG and is home to over 550 native animals including one of the world's largest collections of birds of paradise, tree kangaroos and cassowaries.  ;  ;  Thousands of tropical plant species including palms, gingers and heliconias and native timber trees are found within the grounds.
