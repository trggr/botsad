The Garden was created on February, 22, 1991 by the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian SSR and by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine on October 12, 1992. Geographically it consist of dendropark, dendrariy, arboretum and an ornamental nursery covering the area of 26,05 ha.;  Nowadays, the flora of the Garden includes above 1300 taxons.
