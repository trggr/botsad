Youngken, Jr. Medicinal Plant Garden The garden and greenhouse were established in 1958 and dedicated to Heber W. Youngken, Jr. Dean Emeritus of the College of Pharmacy in 1994. The Garden functions as resource for education and research in medicinal plants and also as a conservatory source of standard specimens. The brick pathways which crisscross the quarter - acre formal style garden provide easy access for visitors to examine the labeled plants.;  Main Address:; Heber W.
