2-Info:              Institution Code: COOK;             International Agenda Registration: No;        BGCI Member: No;                                                  About the Cooktown Botanic Gardens        Heritage listed, the gardens were established in 1868 in the Gallop Botanic Reserve on the edge of Cooktown and contains walking trails to Finch Bay and Cherry Tree Bay. The major collection is the Banks & Solander collection, living specimens of those collected in 1770 by Joseph Banks and Daniel Solander on board the Endeavour with Captain Cook.
