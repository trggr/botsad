The garden is the highest owned by the National Trust nationally. The formal Arts and Crafts gardens (Grade II*), was originally designed by George Dillistone, which are surrounded by architectural yew hedges. The garden contain herbaceous borders, a rose garden. a fragrant garden, a circular Croquet Lawn, a shrubbery and 4 Parrotia arbours under planted with ferns.
