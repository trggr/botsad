2-Info:              Institution Code: BURR;             International Agenda Registration: No;        BGCI Member: No;                                                  About the Burrendong Botanic Garden & Arboretum        Located 25 km from Wellington in the central west of NSW and adjacent to the foreshores of Lake Burrendong, the Burrendong Botanic Garden and Arboretum [BBGA] offers visitors a unique experience. Restricted to Australian natives, the collection dates back to 1964 and includes thousands of plants from all over the continent. The 164 hectares showcase species in bushland settings as well as raised beds, with special emphasis on West Australian and arid land plants.
