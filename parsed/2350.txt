The herbal collection contains about 2.000 pieces, among which several are legally protected. In the greenhouse is a large collection of tropical and subtropical species.;  ;  The Garden has a history of more than 200 years. In the early years it was the medical garden of the nearby Saint Elisabeth hospital. About 125 years ago, important botanical and scientific research was done here. Since 1926, the garden is under the administration of the city council.
