Main mission is to collect, maintain and document colourful collections of plants from all over the world to preserve the wealth of natural species and mutual cooperation among botanic gardens and scientific institutions not only in the Czech Republic but around the world. Apart from wild growing plants the visitor can admire bred varieties of plants. Accessible exposures serve to the visitors for rest and recreation in the cultivated and aesthetic environment.
