These 56 hectare gardens are situated seven kilometres from the city centre.; The Brisbane Botanic Gardens officially opened in 1976. Work to build these gardens at the foot of Mount Coot-tha began in 1970.; ; Brisbane City Council established a second botanic gardens in 1970. The historic City Botanic Gardens was established in 1855 and Sherwood Arboretum was established in 1925.; ; Plant collections at the Brisbane Botanic Gardens Mt Coo-tha include a series of distinctly different gardens arranged in themes and geographical displays.
