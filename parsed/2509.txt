It is the NGRP's responsibility to: acquire, characterize, preserve, document, and distribute to scientists, germplasm of all lifeforms important for food and agricultural production. ; ; The Germplasm Resources Information Network (GRIN) web server provides germplasm information about plants, animals, microbes and invertebrates.
