2-Info:              Institution Code: QFA;             International Agenda Registration: Yes;        BGCI Member: No;                                                  About the Roger-Van den Hende Botanic Garden        The Roger-Van den Hende botanical Garden is Université Laval’s educational garden. Its mission is to serve as a practical laboratory for students and researchers studying and working at Université Laval, particularly at the Faculty of Agricultural and Food Sciences. The garden also has a public education vocation and is a popular open space. ;  ;  This six-hectare educational garden features near than 4000 species and cultivars arranged in order of botanical family. It houses a unique collection of plants native to Quebec and ornamental plants introduced from Europe, America and Asia. It contains a water garden, a collection of herbaceous plants and rhododendrons, an arboretum and a rose garden.;                        Main Address:;         Roger-Van den Hende Botanic Garden;         Pavillon de l'Envirotron, Universite Laval;        2480 Boulevard Hochelaga;        Quebec;        Quebec G1K 7P4 Canada;        ;                 Telephone: (418) 656-3742;         Fax: (418) 656-3515;         URL: www.jardin.ulaval.ca;         Primary Email: jardin@fsaa.ulaval.ca       ;
