2-Info:              Institution Code: ;             International Agenda Registration: No;        BGCI Member: No;                                                  About the Pyunggang Botanical Garden        Located at the entrance of Sanjeong Lake, the Pyunggang Botanical Garden (평강식물원) exhibits both native plants and around 5,000 species of plants from across the globe. The scope of the garden is comprehensive, but it is divided into 12 themed areas, including a Rock Garden, where the largest alpine plants in Asia are concentrated; a Wetland Center, which mimics the natural ecology of a wetland region; a Pond Garden consisting of roughly 50 ponds lined with gorgeous flowers; a Moss Center where moss that grows in deep valleys and forests can be seen; and a Grass Square, which serves as a green common year round.
