Info:	  United Kingdom - Somerset - Ilminster  ;Garden Information;  Institution Code: ; International Agenda Registration: No; BGCI Member: No; About the Barrington Court Barrington Court garden was laid out in the 1920s for the Lyle family to designs by architects J. E. Forbes, who used basket-weave brick paths and fine masonry in walls and outhouses to give the garden structure. Gertrude Jekyll advised on the planting of this varied and enchanting garden laid out in a series of contrasting "rooms". A lovely white garden has been added by the present head gardener, Christine Brain.
