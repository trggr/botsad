Info:	  Thailand - Chiang Mai province - Chiang Mai  ;Garden Information;  Institution Code: QSBG; International Agenda Registration: Yes; BGCI Member: No; About the Queen Sirikit Botanic Garden Queen Sirikit Botanic Garden (QSBG) is located in the mountainous area of Mae Rim District, Chiang Mai Province. The garden was established in 1992 by the Botanical Garden Organization (BGO) under the administration of the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment. In April 1994, The BGO received a great honor from Her Majesty Queen Sirikit to grant her royal permission for Her Majesty's name to be the title of the garden as " Queen Sirikit Botanic Garden" (QSBG).; ; QSBG has 4 satellite gardens, The Rayong Botanic Garden, Rayong Province; Ban Romklao Botanic Garden, Phitsanulok Province; Khon Kaen Botanic Garden, Khon Kaen Province and Phra Mae Ya Botanic Garden, Sukhothai Province.; ; The main objectives of the Garden are to serve as a center of Thai flora for botanical study and research, to render services concerning biodiversity and environmental conservation, as well as to provide an aesthetic place for the general public.; ; The Queen Sirikit Botanic Garden Herbarium (QBG) contains over 100,000 specimens and 53 type specimens, mainly from Thailand and also from other neighboring countries through donating, giving and exchanging, including 113,939 specimens of plant, 6,131 specimens of Bryophytes (Lichens), 44 specimens of algae and 1 specimen of mushroom.
