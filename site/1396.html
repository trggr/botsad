About the Turpan Desert Botanic Garden

Turpan Desert Botanical Garden was built in 1976, Botanical Garden, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 1982 to join the work of the Committee, in 1994, joined the League of Botanic Gardens Conservation International (BGCI); 1999, the China Association for Science named the "national popular science education base" and "National Youth Science and Technology Education Base "; 2006, granted by the National Tourism Administration National AAA-class tourist attractions; 2008, the State Forestry Administration of China Wild Plant Conservation Association awarded the" National Science Education Base Protection of Wild Plants "title.

The park is located southeast of Turpan Basin, the western sandy Cat Kale Township. Geographical coordinates: 40 ° 51'N, 89 ° 11'E, altitude -105 ~-76m.

Currently living plants into germplasm samples Desert Park, the National Medicinal Plant Garden, Desert economic fruit special garden, desert wild ornamental plant garden, desert rare and endangered endemic plant garden, salt desert plant garden, tamarisk plant garden, Calligonum plant garden, garden Calligonum germplasm, Junggar desert areas and arid landscape of grape germplasm resources garden area of 11 special garden (area). Shows a typical arid zone feature and unique characteristics of the temperate flora of wild desert landscape.
