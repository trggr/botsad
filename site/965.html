About the Botanic Garden of St. Petersburg Forestry Academy / ???????????? ??? ?????-????????????? ?????????

Botanical Garden of Saint-Petersburg Forestry Academy is one of the oldest botanical gardens of Russia - it 175 years. Today it is part of a unified system of botanical gardens in Russia and educational research, cultural and educational division of the academy. Botanical Garden was established to preserve, study and enrichment of the gene pool of plants of the natural and cultural flora, as well as providing educational programs of higher and postgraduate education.

Botanical Garden is an object of historical and cultural heritage of federal importance and is a national treasure of Russia. He is registered with the Committee of the State Inspectorate for Protection of Monuments and protected by the state since 1989, as a monument of landscape art in the first half of the nineteenth century with a valuable collection of trees and shrubs.

Since 1995, our Botanical Garden is an environmental object with the direction of the creation of special plant collections for biodiversity conservation and enrichment of flora, as well as implementation of scientific, educational, methodical and educational activities. Botanic Garden is located directly under the Rector SPbGLTA.

The main objectives of the Botanical Garden are:

-provision of highly effective educational process in the development of skilled professionals;
-conservation and plant protection of the natural and cultural flora, plant introduction;
-creation and preservation of collections and exhibitions of living plants that have scientific, educational, economic, cultural and economic significance;
-conduct research consistent with the objectives of the Botanical Garden and the main scientific directions SPbGLTA;
-carry out research and education in botany, ecology and conservation, crop production and breeding, ornamental horticulture, landscape architecture, etc.

The composition of the Botanical Garden includes leading teaching and research work of four laboratories - Laboratory of Amenity Horticulture, Laboratory of woody plants, herbaceous plants in the laboratory and the laboratory of Seed and information support.

Total area of ​​parks and arboreta of 64.96 hectares. The number of species of trees and shrubs 52,525 pcs. (Data from 1985.). In our botanic garden dedicated efforts of many generations of employees with love created the world's richest in terms of North-West collection of plants - more than 1000 species.

We belong to the Council of Botanical Gardens of Russia and working with botanic gardens 400 countries.

Regarding the financing of the Botanical Gardens, then, unfortunately, neither the region (although we are preparing for it professionals), neither region has not identified a single ruble for the maintenance and restoration of our wonderful, unique garden. From the Committee on Urban Environment Park in 1995 was included in the address program for the restoration of ponds, but due to lack of funding the work was stopped. Since 1995, thanks to the city address program building committee of St. Petersburg are working to restore the iron fence. Our park is an open city park and the city should show more attention in that direction.

All of the major concerns of the Botanical garden and park are borne by the Academy.
